About Us

Airrosti gives you the tools and knowledge you need stay pain free! Our videos show you how to perform at-home active rehab exercises and stretches to relieve your pain and prevent future injuries. We also break down and explain the true cause of most common injuries, like knee pain, hip pain, low back pain, and limited mobility, to help you better understand how to prevent them. Our goal is to help you relieve your pain and get you back to doing what you love, fast.
